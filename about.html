All About Me

My name is James Lewis. I started learning coding in the Summer of 2022, and since then I have been working hard to improve on my skills. I went to school at the Mississippi Coding Academy for 11 months. I was taught by the wonderful Mr. Herbert and Ms. Lauren. With their awesome teaching methods, I have become confident in my coding abilities and my work, and I plan to use my capabilites to achieve my dreams.

My dream is to eventually work my way up to becoming a Game developer. After I have gotten enough experience in the field, I plan to eventually move on to working for Nintendo. My end goal is to make my own game for the world to enjoy. I want to inspire all kids, teens, and adults alike to chase their dreams and work towards their future with my games. Everyday I am finding new ways to work towards my goals and build up to my desired future.
